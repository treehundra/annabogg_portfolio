A steel binder clip for clamping the top of a card or photo. Decorative only — position it over the top edge, centered or offset.

```jsx
<div style={{ position: 'relative', paddingTop: 18 }}>
  <BulldogClip tone="black"
    style={{ position: 'absolute', top: -16, left: '50%', transform: 'translateX(-50%)', zIndex: 'var(--z-clip)' }} />
  <ScrapCard>…</ScrapCard>
</div>
```

Finishes: `steel`, `black`. Scale with `size`.
